Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • My verification code didn’t arrive at my @lawrence.edu email.
    Please check your Junk or Spam folder in Outlook—it may have been filtered there.
  • I can't use the "current location" feature.
    Make sure you’ve granted the app permission to access your location *while using the app* in your device settings.
